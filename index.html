71% of Australians agree that the Australian promise has been broken.

For decades Australians have enjoyed a rising standard of living, the expectation of a fair go for all, and a belief that the next generation would enjoy greater opportunity than the last.

The promise to Australians has been broken.

AMPLIFY brings Australians together to confront
hard truths, shape bold solutions, and advocate for change to rebuild the promise — while we still can.

How we work

The promise to Australians has been broken. AMPLIFY's work elevates the community's voice, building political courage to act and rebuild that promise - while we still can.

Explore

Seek

We seek out the best evidence-based reforms from leading experts around the world to the issues that are most important to the Australian community.

Solve

We empower the community to find solutions, using cutting edge tools to debate, deliberate and build consensus.

Spark

We spark the change the community wants to see by advocating to those with the power to make change happen.
